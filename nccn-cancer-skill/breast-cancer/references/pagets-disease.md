# Paget's Disease (PAGET-1) -- Algorithm Reference

<!-- Source: NCCN Breast Cancer v2.2026, pages 109-110 -->
<!-- Chunk: pagets-disease-algorithm -->

## Diagnosis

**Clinical Presentation** [p.109]

Clinical suspicion of Paget disease based on: [p.109]
- Nipple or areolar eczema, ulceration, bleeding, or itching

## Workup

**Initial Assessment** [p.109]

For suspected Paget disease: [p.109]
- Clinical breast exam
- Diagnostic bilateral mammogram, ultrasound as necessary [p.109]
- Assess for distress using NCCN Distress Thermometer and Problem List [p.109]

**Diagnostic Imaging** [p.110]

Two pathways based on imaging findings:

1. **If BI-RADS 4 or 5 imaging** [p.110]
   - Core biopsy of breast lesion and full-thickness skin biopsy of involved nipple-areolar complex (NAC) [p.110]

2. **If examination and imaging negative for breast lesion** [p.109]
   - Full-thickness skin biopsy of involved NAC [p.110]

**Biopsy Interpretation** [p.110]

- **Breast and NAC biopsy negative**: Clinical follow-up; re-biopsy if not healing [p.110]
- **NAC biopsy negative for Paget**: Clinical follow-up; re-biopsy if not healing [p.110]
- **NAC biopsy positive for Paget**: Proceed to staging and treatment planning [p.110]

## Staging / Risk Stratification

**Histopathologic Assessment** [p.110]

Once Paget disease is confirmed, differentiate by breast involvement:

1. **Breast DCIS and NAC Paget** [p.110]
   - Consider breast MRI and tissue sampling, if indicated [p.110]
   - If DCIS or invasive breast cancer: appropriate systemic adjuvant therapy as clinically indicated [p.110]

2. **Breast invasive cancer and NAC Paget** [p.110]
   - Consider breast MRI and tissue sampling, if indicated [p.110]
   - Systemic adjuvant therapy as clinically indicated [p.110]

3. **Breast negative for cancer and positive NAC Paget** [p.110]
   - Proceed to first-line therapy [p.110]

## First-Line Therapy

**Surgical Management** [p.110] (Category 2A) [p.109]

Two surgical options available:

**Option 1: Central Lumpectomy** [p.110]
- Central lumpectomy including NAC [p.110]
- ± Sentinel lymph node biopsy (SLNB) [p.110]
- With whole breast radiation therapy (RT) [p.110]

**Option 2: Mastectomy** [p.110]
- Total mastectomy including NAC [p.110]
- ± SLNB [p.110]
- With or without breast reconstruction [p.110]
- Note: Mastectomy is always an option with any manifestation of Paget disease [p.110]

## Second-Line and Subsequent Therapy

Not specified in algorithm.

## Follow-Up / Surveillance

**Post-Treatment Follow-Up** [p.110]

- Refer to NCCN Guidelines for DCIS or Invasive Breast Cancer for appropriate systemic adjuvant therapy and long-term surveillance [p.110]

---

**Category Assignment**: All recommendations are Category 2A unless otherwise indicated [p.109]
