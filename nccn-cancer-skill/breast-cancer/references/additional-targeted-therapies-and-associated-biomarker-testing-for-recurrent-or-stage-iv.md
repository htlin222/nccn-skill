## Biomarker Testing Notes [p.95-96]

- **ctDNA Testing (u)**: Some plasma-based ctDNA assays measure tumor fraction (TF). Samples with low TF (<1% or negative) should be interpreted with caution. Tests have varying sensitivities at low TF. Additional sampling from current tumor sample or future plasma can be considered. [p.95]

- **ESR1 Mutation Testing (v)**: Assess for ESR1 mutations at progression following prior lines of endocrine therapy (ctDNA preferred). If ESR1 negative and TF is low, consider reflex testing with tissue-based next-generation sequencing (NGS) assay. [p.95]

- **PTEN and TMB Detection (w)**: Tissue biopsy is preferred to ctDNA at detecting homozygous copy loss of PTEN or TMB. [p.95]

- **Disease Progression Context (x)**: Consider for disease progression on adjuvant endocrine therapy or with early disease relapse within 12 months of adjuvant endocrine therapy completion. [p.95]

- **PIK3CA/AKT1/PTEN Mutations (y)**: In adult patients with PIK3CA or AKT1 activating mutations, or for PTEN inactivating mutations and genomic changes (eg, deletion) after disease progression or recurrence after a prior line of endocrine therapy + CDK4/6 inhibitor. [p.95]

- **Aromatase Inhibitor Progression (z)**: For disease progression during or after prior line of aromatase inhibitor plus CDK 4/6 inhibitor therapy in the adjuvant or metastatic setting. [p.95]

- **Aromatase Inhibitor Therapy (aa)**: For disease progression during or after a prior line of aromatase inhibitor with or without CDK 4/6 inhibitor therapy in the adjuvant or metastatic setting. [p.95]

## Additional Drug Approvals and Indications

- **Olaparib**: TBCRC 048 phase II study evaluated olaparib for metastatic breast cancer with mutations in homologous recombination-related genes. [p.96]

- **NTRK Inhibitors** (Entrectinib, Larotrectinib, Repotrectinib): Indicated for solid tumors with NTRK1/2/3 gene fusions without known acquired resistance mutation. Repotrectinib approved for locally advanced or metastatic disease or where surgical resection would result in severe morbidity. First-line recommendation for repotrectinib is Category 2B. [p.96]

- **Pembrolizumab**: Indicated for unresectable or metastatic, microsatellite instability-high (MSI-H) or mismatch repair deficient (dMMR) solid tumors, or TMB-H tumors that have progressed following prior treatment. [p.96]

- **Dostarlimab-gxly**: Indicated for adult patients with MSI-H/dMMR unresectable or metastatic tumors that have progressed on or following prior treatment. [p.96]

- **Selpercatinib**: Indicated for adult patients with locally advanced or metastatic solid tumors with RET gene fusion that have progressed on or following prior systemic treatment. [p.96]
