## Overview

All recommendations are Category 2A unless otherwise indicated [p.44].

## Therapy for Recurrence and Metastasis

### Locoregional Recurrence or Symptomatic Local Disease

#### Endobronchial Obstruction

**No Prior RT:**
- Laser/stent/other surgery [p.44]
- External-beam RT or brachytherapy [p.44]
- Photodynamic therapy [p.44]

**Prior RT:**
- Reresection (preferred) [p.44]
- External-beam RT or SABR [p.44]

#### Mediastinal Lymph Node Recurrence

- Concurrent chemoradiation [p.44] (Category 2A)

#### Superior Vena Cava (SVC) Obstruction

**Any combination of the following:**
- Concurrent chemoradiation (if not previously given) ± SVC stent [p.44]
- External-beam RT ± SVC stent [p.44]
- SVC stent [p.44]

#### Severe Hemoptysis

**Any combination of the following:**
- External-beam RT or brachytherapy [p.44]
- Laser or photodynamic therapy or embolization [p.44]
- Surgery [p.44]

#### Resectable Recurrence

- Concurrent chemoradiation [p.44]

### Distant Metastases

#### No Evidence of Disseminated Disease

- Observation or Advanced/metastatic disease (NSCL-19) [p.44] (Category 2B)

#### Evidence of Disseminated Disease

→ See Advanced/metastatic disease (NSCL-19) [p.44]

### Metastatic Disease by Site

#### Localized Symptoms

- Palliative external-beam RT [p.44]

#### Diffuse Brain Metastases

- Palliative external-beam RT [p.44]

#### Bone Metastasis

- If risk of fracture, orthopedic stabilization + palliative external-beam RT [p.44]
- Consider bisphosphonate therapy or denosumab [p.44]

#### Limited Metastasis

→ Stage IVA, M1b (NSCL-15) [p.44]
→ Advanced/metastatic disease (NSCL-19) [p.44]

#### Pleural Metastases or Disseminated Metastases

→ Advanced/metastatic disease (NSCL-19) [p.44]

## Workup for Advanced/Metastatic Disease

The following studies are recommended [p.44]:
- Chest CT with contrast [p.44]
- Brain MRI with and without contrast [p.44]
- FDG-PET/CT [p.44]

(Note: If MRI is not possible, CT of head with contrast) [p.44]
