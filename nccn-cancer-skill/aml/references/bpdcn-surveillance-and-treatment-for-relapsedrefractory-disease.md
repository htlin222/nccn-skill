## Follow-Up Surveillance

**Category:** All recommendations are Category 2A unless otherwise indicated [p.79]

### Laboratory Monitoring [p.79]

- **CBC, platelets:** Every 1–3 months for 2 years, then every 3–6 months up to 5 years [p.79]
- **BM aspirate and biopsy:** Only if peripheral smear is abnormal or cytopenias develop [p.79]

### Imaging and Biopsy [p.79]

- **Repeat FDG-PET/CT scan:** For patients with prior evidence of extramedullary disease [p.79]
- **Consider re-biopsy:** For any suspicious skin or extramedullary lesions [p.79]

### CNS Assessment [p.79]

- Evaluate CNS for disease/prophylaxis --> See [bpdcn-evaluation-and-treatment-of-cns-disease.md](bpdcn-evaluation-and-treatment-of-cns-disease.md) [p.79]

## Treatment for Relapsed/Refractory Disease [p.79]

### Preferred Options [p.79]

- **Clinical trial** (preferred) [p.79]

### Other Recommended Options [p.79]

- **Tagraxofusp-erzs** (if not already used) [p.79]
  - For management of adverse events, see --> See [bpdcn-principles-of-supportive-care-for-bpdcn.md](bpdcn-principles-of-supportive-care-for-bpdcn.md) [p.79]

- **Chemotherapy** (if not already used) --> See [bpdcn-treatment.md](bpdcn-treatment.md) Treatment Induction (BPDCN-2) [p.79]

- **Local RT** to isolated lesions/areas [p.79]

- **Systemic steroids** [p.79]

- **HMA (azacitidine or decitabine) + venetoclax** --> See [bpdcn-treatment.md](bpdcn-treatment.md) [p.79]

### Donor Search [p.79]

- Donor search should be initiated at first relapse in appropriate patients concomitant with institution of other therapy if no sibling donor has been identified [p.79]
