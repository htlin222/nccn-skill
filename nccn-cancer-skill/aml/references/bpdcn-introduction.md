## Overview

Blastic Plasmacytoid Dendritic Cell Neoplasm (BPDCN) is included in the NCCN Acute Myeloid Leukemia v3.2026 guideline for patients age 18 years and older [p.74].

All recommendations are category 2A unless otherwise indicated [p.74].

## Clinical Management Principles

**Multidisciplinary Approach**: Decisions about diagnosis and management for BPDCN should involve multidisciplinary consultation at a high-volume center with use of appropriate interventions [p.74].

**Academic Referral**: Consider referral to an academic institution [p.74].
