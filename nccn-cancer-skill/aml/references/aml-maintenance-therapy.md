## Maintenance Therapy by Patient Status

### Post-Chemotherapy: Patient with Non-CBF-AML

**Patient Criteria** [p.39]

- Received prior intensive chemotherapy
- Disease is now in remission
- Completed no consolidation or some consolidation
- No allogeneic HCT is planned

**Preferred Treatment** [p.39]

- Consider oral azacitidine until progression or unacceptable toxicity [p.39]
  - Category 1 for age ≥55 years [p.39]
  - Not intended to replace consolidation chemotherapy [p.39]
  - Patients who are fit may benefit from HCT in first CR [p.39]
  - No data suggests maintenance therapy with oral azacitidine can replace HCT [p.39]
  - Trial restricted to patients ≥55 years with intermediate or adverse cytogenetics not felt to be candidates for HCT [p.39]
  - Most patients received at least 1 cycle of consolidation prior to starting oral azacitidine [p.39]
  - Certain circumstances where oral azacitidine may be of benefit for those who completed recommended course of consolidation [p.39]

**Useful in Certain Circumstances** [p.39]

- Azacitidine or decitabine for a maximum of 12 cycles (unable to receive oral azacitidine) [p.39]

**Follow-Up** [p.39]

- Proceed to Surveillance (AML-8) [p.39]

### Post-Chemotherapy: Patient with History of FLT3 Mutation

**Patient Criteria** [p.39]

- Previously received FLT3 inhibitor
- No allogeneic HCT is planned

**Preferred Treatment** [p.39]

- Quizartinib (FLT3-ITD only) [p.39]

**Other Recommended** [p.39]

- Midostaurin (FLT3-ITD or TKD) [p.39]

**Follow-Up** [p.39]

- Proceed to Surveillance (AML-8) [p.39]

### Post-Allogeneic HCT: In Remission with History of FLT3 Mutation

**Preferred Treatment** [p.39]

- Gilteritinib (FLT3-ITD or TKD) [p.39]
  - Preferred for FLT3-ITD in CR1 without MRD negativity by ultrasensitive assay pre-transplant [p.39]

**Other Recommended** [p.39]

- Sorafenib (FLT3-ITD only) [p.39]

- Midostaurin (FLT3-ITD or TKD) [p.39]

- Quizartinib (FLT3-ITD only) [p.39]

**Follow-Up** [p.39]

- Proceed to Surveillance (AML-8) [p.39]

### Post-Allogeneic HCT: In Remission with History of AML with High-Risk Features

**Treatment Option** [p.39]

- Low-dose decitabine + G-CSF (Category 2B) [p.39]

**Follow-Up** [p.39]

- Proceed to Surveillance (AML-8) [p.39]
