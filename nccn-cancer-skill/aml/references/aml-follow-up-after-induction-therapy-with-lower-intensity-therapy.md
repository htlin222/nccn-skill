# AML: Follow-Up After Induction Therapy with Lower Intensity Therapy (AML-5) -- Algorithm Reference

## Assessment After Induction

**BM Assessment** [p.36]

- BM aspirate and biopsy to document remission status (timing dependent on agent) [p.36]
  - For measurable (minimal) residual disease (MRD) assessment, see AML-H [p.36]

## Response-Based Treatment Pathways

### Response (Per Response Criteria, see AML-I) [p.36]

**Preferred Approaches** [p.36]

- Allogeneic HCT [p.36]
  - Patients deemed candidates for HCT with available donor should be transplanted in first remission [p.36]

- Continue on lower intensity regimen that was previously used for induction per AML-4 [p.36]

- Proceed to Maintenance (AML-7) [p.36]

### No Response or Progression (Per Response Criteria, see AML-I) [p.36]

**Treatment Options** [p.36]

- Therapy for Relapsed/Refractory Disease (AML-9) [p.36]

- Best supportive care (hydroxyurea, transfusion support) [p.36]
  - See NCCN Guidelines for Palliative Care [p.36]

## Systemic Therapy Principles

- Principles of Systemic Therapy for AML (AML-E) [p.36]
