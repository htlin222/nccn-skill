# Surveillance (COL-8) -- Evidence Summary

<!-- Source: NCCN Colon Cancer v2.2026, page 16 -->

## Surveillance Recommendations by Pathologic Stage

### Stage I [p.16]

- **History and physical examination:** Every 3-6 months for 2 years, then every 6 months for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **CEA (if patient is a potential candidate for further intervention):** Every 3-6 months for 2 years, then every 6 months for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **Chest/Abdomen/Pelvis (C/A/P) CT:** Every 6-12 months (Category 2B for frequency <12 months) from date of surgery for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **Colonoscopy:** 1 year after surgery except if no complete preoperative colonoscopy, then colonoscopy in 3-6 months [p.16]
  - If advanced adenoma: repeat in 1 year [p.16]
  - If no advanced adenoma: repeat in 3 years, then every 5 years [p.16]
- **FDG-PET/CT:** Not indicated [p.16]
- **Principles of Survivorship:** See COL-H [p.16]

### Stage II and Stage III [p.16]

- **History and physical examination:** Every 3-6 months for 2 years, then every 6 months for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **CEA (if patient is a potential candidate for further intervention):** Every 3-6 months for 2 years, then every 6 months for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **Chest/Abdomen/Pelvis (C/A/P) CT:** Every 3-6 months (Category 2B for frequency <6 months) for 2 years, then every 6-12 months for a total of 5 years [p.16]
- **Colonoscopy:** 1 year after surgery except if no complete preoperative colonoscopy, then colonoscopy in 3-6 months [p.16]
  - If advanced adenoma: repeat in 1 year [p.16]
  - If no advanced adenoma: repeat in 3 years, then every 5 years [p.16]
- **FDG-PET/CT:** Not indicated [p.16]
- **Principles of Survivorship:** See COL-H [p.16]

### Stage IV [p.16]

For patients with stage IV disease managed nonoperatively with complete clinical response:

- **Colonoscopy surveillance:** Initiate colonoscopy surveillance from first documentation of complete response [p.16]
  - If advanced adenoma: repeat in 1 year [p.16]
  - If no advanced adenoma: repeat in 3 years, then every 5 years [p.16]

**Management pathway:** Serial CEA elevation or documented recurrence → See Workup and Treatment (COL-9) [p.16]

## Key Monitoring Parameters

### Advanced Adenoma Definition [p.16]

Advanced adenoma is defined as:

- Villous polyp [p.16]
- Polyp >1 cm [p.16]
- High-grade dysplasia [p.16]

### CEA Monitoring [p.16]

ctDNA is not recommended for surveillance [p.16].

CEA monitoring is only indicated if the patient is a potential candidate for further intervention [p.16].
