## Summary of Key Evidence

- Prior to guideline updates, an electronic search of the PubMed database was performed to obtain key literature using search terms: Breast Cancer, Breast Neoplasms, DCIS, Inflammatory Breast Cancer, OR Phyllodes [p.129]
- PubMed database was chosen as it remains the most widely used resource for medical literature and indexes peer-reviewed biomedical literature [p.129]
- Search results were narrowed by selecting studies in humans published in English [p.129]
- Results were confined to specific article types: Clinical Trial Phase III, Clinical Trial Phase IV, Guideline, Randomized Controlled Trial, Meta-Analysis, Systematic Reviews, and Validation Studies [p.129]
- Data from key PubMed articles selected by the Panel during Guidelines update meetings and articles from additional sources deemed relevant have been included in the Discussion section [p.129]
- Recommendations for which high-level evidence is lacking are based on the Panel's review of lower-level evidence and expert opinion [p.129]
- Complete details of the Development and Update of the NCCN Guidelines are available at www.NCCN.org [p.129]
